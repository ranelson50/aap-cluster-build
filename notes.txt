### AAP download role vars

aap_download_url - The URL used to download the tarball
aap_download_user - Username if downloading the tarball from a URL
aap_download_password -Paswword if downloading the tarball from a URL
offline_token - This is the API token that you can get from logging in at https://access.redhat.com/management/api
